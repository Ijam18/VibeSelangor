# 01 - National Operating Model

## Mission Outcome
Transform technology consumers into AI builders through a staged builder pipeline:
- Stage A: 1-Week Builder Sprint
- Stage B: 8-Week Product Builder Cohort
- Stage C: Founder Lab (invite-only)

## Year 1 Pilot Targets
- Geography: 3-5 states
- Total learners: 1,000
- Live deployments: 500
- Cycles per year: 4
- Learners per cycle: ~250

## Track Allocation Per Cycle
- Juniors: 30% (75 learners)
- Youth: 45% (113 learners)
- Adults/SMEs: 25% (62 learners)

## Stage Progression Gates
### A -> B
Required:
1. Live URL submitted
2. Core user flow works end-to-end
3. Rubric total >= 65

### B -> C
Required:
1. Validated MVP
2. At least 10 user interviews
3. Rubric total >= 75
4. Panel interview pass

## Delivery Language Policy
- Primary instruction in BM
- Technical terms and artifacts can be EN or BM+EN
- All templates and rubrics available bilingually

## Cohort Calendar (Pilot Year)
- Cycle 1: Jan-Mar
- Cycle 2: Apr-Jun
- Cycle 3: Jul-Sep
- Cycle 4: Oct-Dec

## Minimum Infrastructure
- Training venue with projector and stable internet
- Device access model: own laptop or shared lab device
- Cloud stack: deployment + data + analytics tools
- Helpdesk channel for blockers (chat + weekly office hours)

## Governance
- National Curriculum Lead owns standards
- State Program Leads own delivery
- QA Mentors run moderation and rubric calibration
- Industry panel validates Stage C admissions and final demos
