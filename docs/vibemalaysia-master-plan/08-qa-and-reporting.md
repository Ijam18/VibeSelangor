# 08 - QA, Reporting, and Governance

## Reporting Cadence
- Weekly: state ops dashboard (enrollment, deployments, completion, blockers)
- Per cycle: rubric distribution and progression outcomes
- Quarterly: cross-state quality audit and curriculum tuning
- Annual: national impact report mapped to 5-year KPIs

## KPI Framework
## Year 1
- 1,000 learners
- 500 live deployments
- Pilot startup validation pipeline

## Year 3
- 10,000+ annual learners
- University and corporate partnership scale

## Year 5
- 25,000+ annual learners
- 5,000+ live deployments
- 200+ validated startups/year

## Quality Control Protocol
1. Rubric moderation sample of 20% submissions per cycle
2. Cross-state reviewer exchange once per quarter
3. Red-flag escalation for:
   - pass inflation
   - low completion zones
   - repeated safety policy breaches

## Compliance Controls
- Minor consent required for juniors
- AI safety and acceptable-use acknowledgment mandatory
- Data privacy baseline for learner records and submissions

## Implementation Timeline
## P1 (0-90 days)
- finalize curriculum package
- trainer onboarding
- pilot state launch kits

## P2 (90-180 days)
- run first 2 intake cycles
- QA calibration and rubric moderation

## P3 (180-365 days)
- complete 4-cycle year
- automate dashboards
- expand partner network

## P4 (Year 2-3)
- replication playbook to additional states
- institutional integration with universities and enterprise partners
