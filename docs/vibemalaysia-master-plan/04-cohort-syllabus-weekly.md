# 04 - Stage B Syllabus: 8-Week Product Builder Cohort

## Cohort Objective
Turn sprint-level projects into validated MVPs with user evidence and measurable product quality.

## Weekly Plan
## Week 1 - Problem & Success Metrics
- Define user segment and baseline metric
- Deliverables: problem statement v2, success metric sheet

## Week 2 - UX Flow & Design System Basics
- Improve information architecture and interaction clarity
- Deliverables: revised flow map, screen hierarchy, style tokens

## Week 3 - Feature Completion Sprint
- Build must-have features only
- Deliverables: scope freeze and completion board

## Week 4 - Data Integrity & Reliability
- Ensure state/data flows are reliable
- Deliverables: data flow map and failure case checklist

## Week 5 - Validation Interviews
- Run minimum 5 interviews this week
- Deliverables: interview notes, insight synthesis

## Week 6 - Iteration and Product Quality
- Ship improvements from interview insights
- Deliverables: release v0.8 and change log

## Week 7 - Growth Readiness
- Add onboarding, retention hook, and analytics event checks
- Deliverables: funnel assumptions and tracking map

## Week 8 - Demo Day & Progression Review
- Present validated MVP and evidence
- Deliverables: final demo, rubric, progression decision

## Completion Requirements
- Working live MVP
- At least 10 total user interviews
- Rubric total >= 70
