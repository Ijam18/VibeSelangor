# 07 - Staffing and Capacity Model

## National + State Staffing
- 1 National Curriculum Lead
- 1 Program Lead per state
- 1 Track Lead per track (Juniors, Youth, Adults/SMEs)
- Coaches:
  - 1 per 15 learners (Juniors)
  - 1 per 25 learners (Youth/Adults)
- QA Mentor: 1 per 75 learners
- Industry panel: minimum 5 reviewers per cycle

## Capacity Math (Per 250-Learner Cycle)
- Juniors: 75 learners -> 5 coaches
- Youth: 113 learners -> 5 coaches
- Adults: 62 learners -> 3 coaches
- Total coaches per cycle: 13
- QA mentors needed: 4

## Facilitator Enablement
Before each cycle:
1. Rubric calibration workshop
2. Simulation of Day 1 publish flow
3. Troubleshooting protocol drills
4. Safeguarding briefing for minors

## State Launch Checklist
- Venue and connectivity validated
- Device allocation confirmed
- Mentor roster locked
- Support channel live
- Local partner sign-offs complete
